Maintain your investment, even when you can’t be there

Your time is valuable, and so is your real estate investment. Securely ensures the value of your investment is maintained while minimizing the time you spend at your rental property.

Remote access privileges

No more keys to worry about. Securely provides secure keyless access to your property remotely. You control who has access and can change privileges at any time.

Rental app integration

Pre-screened renters can locate your property, take a tour, and apply all without you being there. If you accept them, they can be given full access rights, no key hand-off necessary.

Security camera

Securely monitors your property’s entrance and allows both you and tenants to see and speak with visitors using your mobile device.

Voice-activated maintenance requests

Tenants can tell Securely when maintenace is required and you’ll receive an alert. Either address the issue yourself or Securely will find and contact a certified professional in your area.

Smart appliance sync

Securely syncs with all major smart appliances and alerts you of required maintenance so you can rest assured their won’t be any failures.

Air quality sensors

Securely alerts you of odors like smoke and pets, as well as elevated humidity in your home so you can prevent damage to your investment before it’s too late.

Sometimes a simple reminder is all it takes

We all hope our tenants care as much about maintaining their home as we do. But for those times that they may forget the house rules, Securely sends you alerts so that you can choose to send a gentle reminder to your tenant.
